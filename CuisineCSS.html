Popular Indian dishes

Explore the flavours of India through its diverse and rich culinary traditions!

North Indian Cuisine

North India is known for its rich, aromatic, and flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:
  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spies and meat or vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, tomato-based curry that's a favourote worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

South Indian offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect bland of spices and tangy flavors.

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato.
  • Idli and Sambar: Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew.
  • Chettinand Chicken: A fiery, flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.

South Indian favourite: Masala Dosa

Sweet Delights

No Indian meal is complete without indulging in some mouth-watering desserts:
  • Gulab Jamun: Fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft and spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasam: A South Indian dessert made of milk, rice, and jaggery.
